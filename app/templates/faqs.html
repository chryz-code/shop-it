Introduction

What is Shopit? add

Shopit is an E-commerce platform that equips you with the efficient tools to run your business online(digitally) at your comfort.

Tell me more. add

Shopit serves more than just an e-commerce platform, it also runs as an inventory management system for your goods, growth of sales, orders, customers and many more. It gives accurate and complete details(data) on all transactions made, both incoming funds and withdrawal(payout). It hosts your products on the internet for better reach and communication with customers without any prior technical knowledge or skills.

What can I do on Shopit? add

With Shopit you can get a personalised online store for your business, with a virtual wallet for incoming funds. You get to track how well the business is growing(analytics) and also run sales at anytime with the use of coupon and contacting customers through the reach of your store newsletter. Delivery of parcels is handled by the company itself through partnered logistics services company.

Getting Started

How do I register? add

Visit the Shopit official website and navigate to the registeration page which takes you less than a minute to create an account. For security and validation reasons, you will have to activate and authenticate(log in) your account through a mail that will be sent to the registered email address immediately after signing up.

Product, Sales, Order

How do I add product to my store on Shopit? add

This is a very simple process where you have fill a form, the details of a product. There are prerequsites to add a product, like adding a product category, your bank details etc. AS a firstimer on Shopit, these prerequsites will be shown to you after signing in.

Can I record orders on Shopit? add

Yes, you can. You will get details on all orders made both paid and unpaid. You also have the privilege to know if the order owner is a customer of the store, and you will recieve a mail after a paid order and many more.

How do I set up logisitics on Shopit? add

This is a burden off store owners, Shopit has a logistics integration to it's official partnered logistics services company for pickup and delivery of parcels and goods all over the nation.

How do I know a product has been purchased? add

You will get a notifications of the order and it's details (eg. the products purchased), a mail. You also verify through transactions made to your virtual wallet or the store analytics.

Transanction Charges

Does Shopit take payment charges from the merchents? add

Yes, Shopit charges a specific percentage of the transactions made by merchants based on the subscription plan of the store and the charges can not be more than ₦2500.
  • Free Plan: 2%
  • Standard Plan: 1%
  • Professional Plan: 0%
The transanction charges of the payments gateway(api) user will be automatically added to products prices.

How do I get payment made on the store? add

A store on shopit is entitled to a virtual wallet, either you generate it or it genrates while payment is been made to the store. This wallet runs mainly with the Paystack payout system/policy, only that you have to withdraw it yourself. After every verified payment, the wallet will be funded automatically but here is the policy;
  • Withdrawal can not be made on weekends and public holidays
  • Only funds recieved 24 hours earlier can be withdrawn
  • You can't withdraw any amount less than ₦1000(Shopit)
More importantly, you'll need to put your bank account details in the store account settings where verified funds will be transferred to in less than an hour.
